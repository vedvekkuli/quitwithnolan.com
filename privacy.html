Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

Nothing personal. All your data (quit timer, progress, preferences) stays on your device. We don't see it, store it, or transmit it.

Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data (screens viewed, session length) to improve the app. This data cannot identify you. No names, emails, or device identifiers are collected.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store or Google Play. We never see your payment information.

Health information

Nolan does not collect or store health data. The app provides educational information about nicotine withdrawal and is not a medical device.

Motion data

Nolan uses your device's motion sensors to detect a shake gesture (for craving support). This data is processed on-device only and is never stored or transmitted.

Third-party services

We may use third-party services for analytics and subscription management. These services have their own privacy policies and do not receive personally identifiable information from Nolan.

Children

Nolan is not intended for anyone under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes are reflected in the date above.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at hello@quitwithnolan.com.
